## 🚀 The Future of Professional Identity

Your professional identity shouldn't be scattered across **dozens of PDFs, stale LinkedIn pages, and unstructured project docs**. When recruiters have only **30 seconds** to evaluate you, a static resume fails to tell your real story. 

**IdentityOS is the operating system for your career.**

We built an AI-native platform that automatically syncs, structures, and maps your professional life into a high-dimensional knowledge graph—powering a suite of intelligent career tools.

---

## ✦ Core Engine

1. **Multi-Modal Ingestion** — Drop in resumes, certs, GitHub URLs, or project PDFs.
2. **AI Extractor** — GPT-4o powered entity extraction (skills, dates, roles, organizations, impact metrics).
3. **Living Knowledge Graph** — Maps everything with GNN (Graph Neural Network) confidence scores.
4. **Career Twin** — Your dynamic digital AI persona synthesized from verified evidence.
5. **Proactive AI Copilot** — Analyzes your graph to predict career gaps, salary trajectories, and optimal learning roadmaps.
6. **Recruiter Microsite** — Exports a stunning, one-click shareable interactive portfolio.

---

## 🧠 Autonomous Intelligence Modules

- **Mission Control Dashboard**: See your Identity Ring, Recruiter Gauge, and Learning Velocity at a glance.
- **Explainability Center**: Trace every AI inference back to the exact chunk of verified evidence. Zero hallucinations.
- **Career Simulator**: Turn the dials on your experience and watch your projected salary, role probabilities, and GNN skill topography recalculate in real-time.
- **AI Resume Lab**: Automatically scan for missing ATS keywords and transform weak qualitative bullets into quantified, high-impact statements with one click.
- **Presentation Mode**: A clean, distraction-free Recruiter View built for sharing.

## 🏗️ Architecture Stack

### Frontend (Next.js 14 App Router)
- **Visuals**: Framer Motion, Custom SVG micro-animations, Glassmorphism
- **Graphing**: ReactFlow v11
- **State**: React hooks + Context

### Backend (Python)
- **API Engine**: FastAPI + Uvicorn
- **AI Pipeline**: LangChain + GPT-4o
- **Knowledge Graph**: Neo4j 5
- **Vector Search**: Qdrant
